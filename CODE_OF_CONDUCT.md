# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in AGU welcoming and harassment-free for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific in technical discussions.
- Assume good intent while accepting correction.
- Protect private video, identity data, credentials, and unpublished datasets.
- Attribute external code, research, data, and model contributions accurately.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, doxxing, publishing private media,
or knowingly misrepresenting model accuracy or licensing is unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct issues privately to repository maintainers. Maintainers may edit,
remove, or reject contributions and restrict participation when necessary.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
